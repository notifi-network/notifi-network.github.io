Custom Email domain

First thing you want to do is decide on your custom domain. If you already send email and do business from example.com, where your email would look like the following format: info@example.com, we suggest you don't try to send from the same domain. Choose a subdomain, that can be anything you want. Having a domain such as mail.example.com would then have email sent from info@mail.example.com Once you have the domain chosen, navigate to your Account Settings in Notifi Admin Portal.

Click Account Settings​

Click Add Domain​

Enter your domain​

Set your DNS records​

After 72 hours, the domain will go into a failed state, and you’ll need to delete and start over.
